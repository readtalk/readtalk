Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about READTalk

Yes, READTalk is completely free to use. You can send unlimited messages, make voice and video calls, and share files without any cost. We don't show ads or charge subscription fees.

READTalk uses end-to-end encryption for all messages, calls, and shared media. This means only you and the person you're communicating with can read or listen to what's sent. Not even READTalk can access your messages.

Absolutely! READTalk syncs across all your devices - phone, tablet, computer, and web browser. Your conversations, contacts, and media are automatically synchronized so you can seamlessly switch between devices.

You can share photos, videos, documents (PDF, Word, Excel, etc.), audio files, contacts, and your location. Files up to 2GB can be shared, and all shared content is encrypted for your privacy.

Creating a group chat is easy! Tap the new chat icon, select 'New Group', choose your contacts, add a group name and photo, and you're ready to go. Groups can have up to 256 participants.

Yes, READTalk automatically backs up your chats to your cloud storage (iCloud for iOS, Google Drive for Android). You can also manually export chat histories and restore them when switching devices.

READTalk requires an internet connection (Wi-Fi or mobile data) to send and receive messages. However, you can still view your previous conversations and media when offline.

You can report and block unwanted contacts directly from the chat. Tap on the contact's name, select 'Report' or 'Block', and choose the appropriate reason. Our team reviews all reports promptly.
